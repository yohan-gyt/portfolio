As part of the audiovisual module in DUT MMI, I had to make a video cv in English in order to present myself in a more original and personal way than on paper. I decided here to show the montage I made, which also happens to be my first montage.
Doing a montage that represents me was a difficult task, my initial idea having been disrupted by the announcement of the second containment, I had to quickly shoot shots without having any idea what I would do with them. So I decided to show my creativity by making a video editing inspired by music videos from mostly hazardous shots.
