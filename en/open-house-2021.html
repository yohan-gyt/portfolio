As part of the module of aesthetics and artistic expression in DUT MMI we had to realize in group an institutional campaign of our training for the open doors and my role was to realize the poster.
We had decided to reproduce the poster from the film Parasite by replacing the characters with students representing the fields of the MMI training. Their eyes being hidden, the JPO visitors could identify themselves more easily.
I also made the illustration on the graphic designer’s iPad.
